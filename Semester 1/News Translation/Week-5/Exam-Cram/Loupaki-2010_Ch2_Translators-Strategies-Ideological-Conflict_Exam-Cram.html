üîç FRAMING DEVICES (Tannen & Wallat 1993)

3 Main Devices:

  1. Interpretative naming: Words chosen to describe persons/objects/situations
  2. Evaluative language: Adjectives/adverbs showing speaker's opinion
  3. Modals: Reflect judgment by speaker's standards
Al-Qaeda Framing Example:
  • NYT: "organization," "network," "operatives" (neutral)
  • Economist: "terrorists," "jihadists," "militants" (charged)
  • Kathimerini BEFORE bombing: "organization," "members"
  • Kathimerini AFTER bombing: "extremists," "fundamentalists," "horrifying organization"

üí° VAN DIJK'S IDEOLOGICAL SQUARE

Argumentation Strategy in Political Discourse:
  1. Emphasize OUR good actions
  2. De-emphasize OUR bad actions
  3. Emphasize THEIR bad actions
  4. De-emphasize THEIR good actions
